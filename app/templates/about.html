ABOUT

What is 311 Labs?

A space where your dreams of the possiblities of 311 data can become a reality! We're here to experiment and play, but most importantly our goal is to help inform citizens and cities along the way.

What is 311?

We're not referring to the band from the 90s, it's the number you call to report non-emergency service requests from your city. Examples of non-emergency service requests include: potholes, grafitti removal, street lights are out, tree trim, etc.
